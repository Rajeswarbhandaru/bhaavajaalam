About Bhāva Tech

IQ and cognitive enhancement centre

The human brain is the most powerful organ in the known universe — yet most people never truly understand how it works or how to use it to its full potential. At Bhāva Tech, we help children, students, and adults do exactly that.

Through scientifically designed IQ, EQ, and SQ assessments, we give you a clear picture of where your cognitive strengths lie and where improvement is possible. Our Brain Gym programs train the mind the same way physical exercise trains the body — with consistency, structure, and measurable results. Our Mindfulness & Meditational Lab creates a dedicated space for mental clarity, stress relief, and focused thinking — something every modern learner urgently needs.

  • 🧠 Understand your IQ, EQ & SQ with certified assessments

  • 🧘 Restore focus and calm in our Meditational Lab
  • 📊 Receive detailed personal reports with actionable guidance

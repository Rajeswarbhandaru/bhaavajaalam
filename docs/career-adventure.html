✅ Before You Decide

Honest questions to ask yourself
  • What do I genuinely enjoy doing for hours without getting bored?
  • What am I naturally good at — not just what I'm pushed to do?
  • Which subjects energize me and which drain me?
  • What is my family's financial situation and how does it affect my options?
  • Do I prefer to study more years, or start working sooner?
  • Do I want to work in India, abroad, or be open to both?
  • Do I want a government job, corporate career, business, or creative work?
  • Have I talked to someone already working in the field I'm considering?

👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents

Support without pressure
👂

Listen First

Understand the student's real interests before suggesting a career path.

🚫

Avoid Comparisons

Every student develops differently. Neighbor's child's path is not your child's path.

🌱

Think Long-Term

Focus on growth, skill, and wellbeing — not just marks or prestige.

💬

Talk to Experts

Visit a career counselor, alumni, or working professional before finalizing a path.

📊

Consider Finances Honestly

Discuss fees, loans, and scholarships openly. No career is worth lifelong debt stress.

🔄

Paths Can Change

A career change at 25 or 30 is normal. One choice at 16 does not seal a whole life.

Career is a direction, not a prison. One score does not decide a whole life. 🌱

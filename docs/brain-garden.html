Parent tips

  • Sit beside your child for the first round of each game.
  • Praise effort, not just right answers.
  • Pattern Path builds logical reasoning — talk through the rule together.
  • Word Rhyme supports early reading and phonics awareness.
  • Two sessions a day of 5–10 minutes is ideal.

➕ Addition

Add means put together.Start from the ones place. Add digits column by column. Carry over if sum ≥ 10.34 + 28 = 62  |  1,200 + 800 = 2,000
Adding with 0:Any number + 0 = that number itself.57 + 0 = 57

➖ Subtraction

Subtract means take away.Start from ones. Borrow from the next column if needed.52 − 17 = 35  |  1000 − 250 = 750
Subtracting 0:Any number − 0 = that number.89 − 0 = 89

✖ Multiplication

Multiply means repeated addition.4 × 3 = 4 + 4 + 4 = 12. Order doesn’t matter: 3 × 4 = 4 × 3.7 × 8 = 56  |  12 × 5 = 60
Multiply by 10, 100, 1000:Add 1, 2, or 3 zeros to the right.7 × 10 = 70  |  7 × 100 = 700  |  7 × 1000 = 7000

÷ Division

Divide means share equally.20 ÷ 4 = 5 means share 20 into 4 equal groups.48 ÷ 6 = 8  |  100 ÷ 5 = 20
Division by 1:Any number ÷ 1 = itself.63 ÷ 1 = 63

🔢 Place Value & Numbers

Place Value Chart (Indian System):Ones | Tens | Hundreds | Thousands | Ten-Thousands | Lakhs | Ten-Lakhs | Crores35,72,046 = 3 Crores + 5 Ten-Lakhs + 7 Lakhs + 2 Ten-Thousands + 46
Rounding Rules:Look at the digit to the right. If it is 5 or more, round up. If less than 5, round down.347 rounded to nearest 100 = 300  |  650 rounded to nearest 100 = 700
